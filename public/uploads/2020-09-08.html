ELYSIUM CAPITAL WEEKLY UPDATE the 8th of SEPTEMBER 2020
 

The Past & Highlights
 

US labor market stronger than expected

COVID-19 has had profound impact on people's life, economies and financial markets. And will likely continue to do so for a considerable period. Investors are still betting on an effective vaccine that will help overcome the crisis soon. 

At the end of the last week, we saw large declines in the stock markets. The American tech giants, whose stock prices rose sharply during the year, turned down with companies such as Apple and Tesla at the forefront. The VIX index, which measures stock market volatility, is now back at June levels, but still well below its peak in March.

The week ended with US labor market statistics. The number of new employees was just under 1,4 million people, largely as expected. The big surprise was unemployment, which landed at 8,4 percent compared to the expected 9,8 percent.

In the United States, the Purchasing Managers' Index for Industry rose further driven by an improved order intake, the most forward-looking indicator. However, the plans of hiring new labor are still subdued, which indicates that the recovery will take time.

The US presidential election is approaching and new polls figures are being released every day. According to the latest figures, Biden still has a clear lead, but the betting firms indicate only a marginal advantage. Biden is ahead in the national and swing states polls. Among the swing states, the most important to follow is Florida, as it is very difficult for Trump to get re-elected without Florida. Texas has also become more competitive. Prediction markets believe the probability of a Biden victory  is around 55%.
 

EA Portfolio Performance

Elysium AURUM DIGITAL portfolio had a strong performance so far in September with a net return of around 0,72% (adjusted for performance fees). Main contributions were from gold (XAUUSD), but all pairs contributed with positive returns so far for the current month. 

Elysium PINNACLE portfolio is up net by around 2,07% (adjusted for performance fees) The portfolio mainly traded USDJPY and USDCAD around 30% of the turnover each, closely followed by the EURUSD pair. Best risk/reward attribution came from EURUSD trading, with a fifty/fifty split of long and short positions over the month so far. 

Elysium ALPHA LINEA portfolio has mostly traded during the month so far in EURUSD followed by the EURNZD pair. The portfolio has a net increase by 1,53% (adjusted for performance fees) in September so far. 
 

Currency Markets

The spread of COVID-19 is currently increasing again in Spain, France and Italy and this increase the downside of the GDP for the EURO zone. So far, the EURO zone economies had taken a bigger hit then the Nordic economies due to how they are composed, the possibility to work from home and the level of lockdown applied. Countries with lower budget deficits and dept to GDP ratios like Sweden, Germany and Netherlands has better conditions to counter the worsening conditions then others. 

USD, the US presidential election compounds two key dimensions: tax and trade (China). If Democrats win both the presidency and the both chambers in Congress, it would be the most USD-negative scenario, while a clear Republican victory would be the most USD-positive outcome. But in the near-term future there are more important drivers for EURUSD. For example the September FOMC meeting, the ECB's view on EUR strength, global activity, equity markets and the potential for US fiscal easing. 

GBP, Cable (GBPUSD) selling pressure intensifies as the pair drop below 1.3200. UK Chief negotiator David Frost said Britain isn't scared of walking away empty handed and fears of no-deal Brexit increase.
 
GBPUSD 30 minutes 5 days chart
 
 
Commodities Gold (XAUUSD) continues to bounce at the trendline support from the March low, currently around USD 1930, with the risk of an extension to USD 1900 if break of support. The market has been range bound while watching the dollar, real yields and stock market developments for clues about the direction. A rapid sell-off in stocks may pose a challenge with gold and silver (XAGUSD) becoming a source of liquidity to cover margin calls and losses elsewhere.
