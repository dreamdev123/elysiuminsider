ELYSIUM CAPITAL WEEKLY UPDATE the 14th of DECEMBER 2020


The Past & Highlights

Equities was down last week, where real estate and financials depreciated the most. The stock market was negatively affected by the job market that appears to be recovering slower than expected and talks between Republicans and Democrats over another round of fiscal stimulus continue to be delayed. A further postponed stimulus package is negative for stocks as further economic restrictions from rising corona cases constrict growth. Workers in U.S. claiming unemployment insurance for the first-time exceeded consensus estimates but consumer sentiment posted a surprising increase in December which gave some support to equities.



S&P 500 daily chart
 

Outlook

Stock indexes reached fresh record highs last week before falling back to finish modestly lower, as stalled Washington stimulus talks and the likelihood for a no-deal Brexit soured the optimistic spirit.
 
The market looks to be focusing on a brighter outlook driven by the successful launch and distribution of corona vaccines across the world. Even due recent coronavirus trends continue to worsen and restrictions in activity are reimposed. Since the start of the year S&P500 have printed 30 new all-time highs, where 17 of them occurred after the late February pandemic mini crash. The question is if the market not is slightly over optimistic, forgetting about possible setbacks in 2021? At least the upside is more capped going forward. 
 

EA Portfolio Performance

Elysium AURUM DIGITAL portfolio EA is up net of around 1,91% (adjusted for performance fees) for December, month to date. The main contributors were from trading in EURJPY and NZDUSD. But most pairs contributed with positive returns exempt for the EURCAD cross, for the current month.

Elysium PINNACLE portfolio EA had a good performance the past week and is now up net by around 2,73% (adjusted for performance fees) month to date. In December, the portfolio has mainly traded USDCAD representing around 18% of the volume followed by EURJPY and NZDJPY representing around 13% respectively 12% of the traded volume in December. Other pairs traded frequently was EURCAD and EURAUD. Best risk/reward attribution was from NZDJPY followed by USDCAD so far for the current month. 

After a slow start of the month the Elysium ALPHA LINEA portfolio EA has strongly performed the past week and are now up net by around 4,30% (adjusted for performance fees) for December, month to date. Trading in NZDJPY has mainly contributed to the return the past week, but also AUDJPY, GBPUSD and GBPJPY contributed to the positive performance. Mostly traded pairs were the majors GBPUSD and EURUSD with both approximately 22% of the traded volume for December so far.
 

Currency Markets
 
Sterling (GPB) was in a sky dive mode last week after that UK and EU stated that the likely hood of a no-deal Brexit has increased. The former deadline was set to Sunday but will now be extended again. Commodity currencies was the strong currencies last week, in particular the Aussie Dollar (AUD) fueled by the increase in iron ore price. Canadian dollar (CAD) was also strong, supported by raising oil prices. Also New Zealand dollar (NZD) and the Swiss Franc (CHF) had a strong week. 

This week we look at the more volatile GBPCHF pair. We are currently range bound in a longer sideways trading channel established in April this year. We see that the cross is testing the secondary falling trading channel within the longer sideways channel. With Brexit deal negotiations in mind, it is difficult to trade the any breakout of the downwards channel established in early December with confidence. 
 


GBPCHF daily chart
 

Commodities 
 
Gold (XAUUSD) has been back and forth during the past week testing the resistance around 1880 USD/ounce level. Gold pull back probably because there is not any progress with regards to a new stimulus package in the US.  Once a new package is more clarified probably it will have a positive effect on gold. Meanwhile it might probably just stay within this falling trading channel established since august this year.
